What we do

Hearing care for every stage of life

From your first hearing test to long-term care of your devices, our audiologists and licensed specialists walk with you every step.

Diagnostic Hearing Evaluations

Thorough, painless testing that maps the full picture of your hearing and shapes the right treatment plan.

Learn more

Treatment of Hearing Loss

Personalized, evidence-based care to protect cognition, relationships, and quality of life.

Learn more

Hearing Aid Consultation, Fitting & Repairs

Fittings you can actually hear the difference in — plus real-ear verification, repairs, and maintenance.

Learn more

Assistive Listening Devices

TV streamers, remote mics, captioned phones, and apps that extend your hearing beyond the aids themselves.

Learn more

Auditory Training

Targeted exercises that strengthen the ear-to-brain connection — because hearing is rebuilt in practice, not just in devices.

Learn more

Redux® Professional Drying

In-office moisture removal that verifiably revives devices in minutes — part of our standard of care.

Ear Impressions & Earmolds
