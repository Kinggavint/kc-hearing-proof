Meet the team

Your audiologists, specialists, and support team.

Dedicated. Experienced. Patient-centered. Every provider at KC Hearing Center builds a plan of care that fits your hearing, your life, and your goals.

Audiologists & specialists

Clinical team

Kathryn Grote, MS

Audiologist · Owner
All locations

Kathy is an audiologist with a passion for helping people by improving their hearing. A former leading regional representative for two global hearing aid manufacturers, she assisted patients across the Midwest before realizing the need for local, community-rooted care — and founding KC Hearing Center. With 20 years of experience in audiology, Kathy has developed a deep understanding of hearing aid technology and the impacts of untreated hearing loss. Outside the clinic, she enjoys family time, weekends at the lake, and attending her two sons' sporting events.

Dr. Pamela A. Nelson, Au.D.

Audiologist
Independence & Raytown

Pamela has over 30 years of experience working with patients to improve their hearing and communication skills. She received her Doctor of Audiology from Salus University and has practiced in private offices and ENT clinics. A past President of the Kansas City Society of Audiology and Convention Co-Chair of the Missouri Speech and Hearing Association, she takes a holistic approach — because better hearing improves communication with family, friends, and co-workers, which improves quality of life.

Dr. Haley Womack, Au.D.

Audiologist
Raytown, Independence, Higginsville & Belton

Haley graduated in May 2023 with her Doctor of Audiology from Missouri State University. Her clinical experience includes vestibular testing, tinnitus treatment, and hearing aid fittings. She believes in a patient-centered plan of care and takes a personalized approach to selecting the hearing aid that best suits each individual's lifestyle — because better hearing can profoundly change a person's outlook on life.

Dr. Minnie Baldridge, AuD, CCC-A

Audiologist
Raytown & Butler

Minnie has her doctorate in audiology and over 20 years of experience across the Kansas City region. She has worked in hospitals, private practice, ENT clinics, and directly with insurance companies, and she serves veterans by performing C&P evaluations. Minnie offers a kind, personalized approach to every visit, with a goal of increasing access to hearing health care for all.

Kara Barrus, BA

Licensed Hearing Instrument Specialist
Belton & Raytown

Kara's approachable and compassionate nature makes her a trusted partner in improving hearing health. When she is not providing patient care, Kara enjoys spending time with her husband, family, and friends — embracing lake life, being outdoors, and playing golf.

Dr. Stacey Kampe, Au.D., CCC-A

Audiologist
Wichita

Dr. Stacey Kampe has served the Wichita community for more than 30 years. She began her career at Heartspring, then practiced with Via Christi ENT before joining Wichita State University as a clinical supervisor and professor. Her clinical passions include hearing aids, tinnitus, hyperacusis, and noise-induced hearing loss prevention — all delivered with whole-person care and practical, compassionate guidance.
